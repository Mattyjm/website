Fablab Safety


On the first week, we learnt about the Fablab Safety, and did the Fablab Safety Quiz.

Proper Attire (applicable to other labs):

1. T-shirt and full-length trousers.
2. Do not wear loose-sleeved clothing and jewellery.
3. No loose items (jewellery) allowed.
4. Covered shoes must be worn at ALL time â€“ NO open tops or open toes
5. Students with long hair must tie up their hair to avoid entanglement with rotating machinery.
6. Certain laboratories and workshops require the use of additional safety equipment to protect against any workplace and machine hazards.

Rules:

1. No eating and drinking in the in laboratories and workshops
2. Phones must be switched off while working in laboratories and workshops
3. Observe and abide all warning signs and safety notices.
4. Be familiar with the location of first-aid box, safety eye-wash and /or shower, chemical spill kit within each laboratory and workshop
5. Know the location of fire escape exits and assembbly areas.
6. No foul play in labs/ workshops.
7. Refer/ adhere to safe operating or work procedures.
8. Do not operate machines or equipment unless properly instructed
9. Keep your work area clean before leaving the laboratory/workshop.
10. Report any UNSAFE conditions or UNSAFE acts to your lecturer-in-charge.
